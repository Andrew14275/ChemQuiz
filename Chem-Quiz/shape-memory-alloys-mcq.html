Quick idea

Shape memory alloys can return to a pre-set shape after deformation when temperature changes trigger a reversible phase transformation.

Why engineers use them

They combine compact actuation, damping, biocompatibility in NiTi, and strong recovery behavior, which makes them useful in medical devices and smart mechanisms.
